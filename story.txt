P135
The tunnel continues north until at last you arrive at a junction. If you wish to crawl west, turn to 284. If you wish to crawl east, turn to 151.

P284
You soon arrive at another junction in the tunnel. If you wish to head north, turn 81. If you wish to head south, turn to 270. OR Secret option 105.

P270
The tunnel ends at a cave entrance. On entering the cave you see that the roof is no higher than the tunnel and you are unable to stand up. The cave is the lair of an intelligent creature, for it contains small pieces of furniture. There is a wooden box just over a metre in length at the back of the cave.
If you wish to lift the lid of the box, turn to 229.
If you wish to turn round and return to the junction in the tunnel, turn to 121.

P151
The tunnel ends at a cave entrance. A curtain hangs over the entrance and you are unable to see inside. If you wish to throw back the curtain and enter the cave, turn to 71. If you wish to turn round and return to the juntion int the tunnel, turn to 296.

P105
In the distance to the right of the path you see large birds circling in the sky. As you get closer you recognize them as vultures. If you wish to step off the path to see what or whom the vultures are interested in, turn to 384. If you wish to ignore the vultures and continue walking north, turn to 394.

P81
Ahead you hear high pitched voices frantically calling each other. The tunnel ends at a small cave entrance. Suddenly an arrow flies out of the cave towards you. Test your luck. If you are Lucky, the arrow whistles oast your head (turn to 49). If you are Unlucky, it lodges in your shoulder (turn to 4).

P296
Back at the junction you may either crawl south back tot he well(398) or continue west(284)
