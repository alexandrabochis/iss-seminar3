P135
The tunnel continues north until at last you arrive at a junction. If you wish to crawl west, turn to 284. If you wish to crawl east, turn to 151.

P151
The tunnel ends at a cave entrance. A curtain hangs over the entrance and you are unable to see inside. If you wish to throw back the curtain and enter the cave, turn to 71. If you wish to turn round and return to the jucntion int the tunnel, turn to 296.
